üìö Health Tips for Donors

ü•ó

Before Donation

  • Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours) the night before
  • Eat a healthy, iron-rich meal 2-3 hours before donation
  • Drink plenty of water (at least 500ml) before donation
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before donation
  • Bring ID and donor card if you have one
üíß

After Donation

  • Rest for 10-15 minutes after donation
  • Drink extra fluids for next 24-48 hours
  • Avoid heavy lifting or strenuous exercise for 5 hours
  • Eat iron-rich foods for the next few days
  • Keep the bandage on for at least 4 hours
‚ù§Ô∏è

Benefits of Regular Donation

  • Reduces risk of heart disease and cancer
  • Helps in weight management
  • Rejuvenates blood cell production
  • Saves up to 3 lives per donation
  • Provides free health check-ups
